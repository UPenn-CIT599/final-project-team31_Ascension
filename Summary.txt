Project Name: Ascension 
Authors: Jiayu Vivienne Zhu, Shari Sinclair and David Cai 

Description: 
  Ascension is a turn-based RPG in which the player aims to climb a 10 floor tower to reach the treasure. The player has the freedom to choose from one of the three classes, Warrior, Mage or Rogue. Through strateigc uses of the attack, defend, and heal functions, the player needs to defeat the enemies on each floor in order to proceed to a higher floor. The player can purchase potion items and revive at the magic shop and has the probabilities to run into mysterious events. On the 10th floor, there will be a Boss enemy and the player has to defeat it in order to win the game.
